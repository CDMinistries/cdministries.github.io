Week of March 16, 2026 — Walking in Faith

“Trust in the Lord with all your heart, and lean not on your own understanding.” — Proverbs 3:5

This week, let us be reminded that God’s plan is often bigger and more complex than what we can see in the moment. When the path ahead seems uncertain, choose to trust in Him, knowing that His wisdom far exceeds our own. Faith is not believing that God will do what *we* want—faith is trusting that He will do what is right.
